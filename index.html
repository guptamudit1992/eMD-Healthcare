Features

It's easier than it sounds! Carry your complete health record, use reminders and trackers to stay on top of every detail.

Healthbook

Maintain a comprehensive record of your medical details and history on hand. You can share with it doctors both online and offline.

Notification System

Raise alarms well before medical contingencies based on our system intelligence

Secured Cloud Storage

Data is stored right on your device, and on secured cloud servers. Only you can authorize and share your medical information when you want. So you don't need to worry about who can see your data at the other end of the line.

Digital Vault

eMD360 provides a vault for x-rays, medical recommendations, bills, lab reports etc. that can be attached to your record and shared in the future.

Track Vitals

Track your blood pressure, glucose level and body mass index via graphs

Medical Information Portal

eMD360 offers a single point source for all health related information you would ever need. Standard and updated health information from US National Library of Medicine, simplified from complex medical terminology into easy to understand language especially for targeted patient education.
